```
- I just started a couple of weeks ago.

    我几周前刚开始工作

- Well, if there is anything I can do for you, let me know.

    如果有什么我能帮忙的，尽管开口

```

```
- When would you like me to send those plans over to your office?

    你要我什么时候把这些计划送到你的办公室？

- It would be good if you could get them to me by Thursday afternoon.

    你最好能在周四之前把他们交给我
```

```
- That'll be ahead of schedule! Good work.

    这样就比计划提前了

- Well, as the saying goes: we aim to please!

    常言道：我们的目标就是使人满意
```

```
- I was wondering if you finished that presentation you were working on last week.

    我想知道你是否完成了上周正在做的演讲。

- Just about. It should be done in the next couple of days.

    快了。应该在接下来的几天内完成。
```

```
- Hey, why do not we grab a bite to eat and do some catching up?

    嘿，我们是不是去吃点什么，好好聊聊天？

- Yeah, why not? I've got a little free time on my hands.

    为什么不呢？我正好有点时间
```
